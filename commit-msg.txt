fix: CronEngine cwd crash when launched from Claude Desktop

TaskManager used process.cwd() to locate data/cron.db. When Claude Desktop
spawns the MCP server, cwd is C:\Windows\System32 (read-only) causing an
EPERM mkdir crash on startup.

Fix: derive cronDbPath from import.meta.url so it always resolves to
D:\Dev\oktyv\data\cron.db regardless of the calling process cwd.

Added: fileURLToPath and path imports to server.ts
